I am an incoming PhD student at the Language Technology Lab of the University of Cambridge (starting in Fall 2023) and an intern at Cohere. I do research in Natural Language Processing. Right now, I am especially interested in multilinguality, tokenization and language emergence. I am also interested in Rust as a language for writing fast, correct research code. I obtained a BSc in Artifical Intelligence from Johannes Kepler University Linz in 2023. Previously, I interned at H2O.ai and Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab in London. I started out by being active on Kaggle.
